Last updated 2014-01-01.

Areas

The visited areas are based on a map called "Kartat ja Kadunnimet P‰‰kaupunkiseutu" (ISBN: 951-98617-1-8, printed in 2001):

  • Scale 1:20 000
  • KKJ coordinate system
  • 48 A4 pages (210 × 297 mm)
  • Each page covers 4 × 5 = 20 square kilometers (200 × 250 mm)
  • In total the map covers 48 × 20 = 960 square kilometers
  • The map covers:
    • Helsinki (exluding some islands and the expansion to Sipoo in 2009)
    • Most parts of Espoo
    • Vantaa
    • Kauniainen
    • Small parts of Kirkkonummi, Vihti, Nurmij‰rvi, Tuusula, Kerava and Sipoo

Extensions

The map was extended by:

  • 20 pages (A-T) in 2009 to cover also all of Helsinki and Espoo
  • 7 pages (a-g) in 2010 to cover all of Ulkoilukartta
  • 12 pages (h-s) in 2011
  • 8 pages (49-56) in 2012
  • 10 pages (57-66) in 2013
  • 14 pages (67-80) in 2014

The extensions can be shown/hidden using the left-click context menu.

Trips

The trips are recorded with GPS logger i-gotU GT-120 (marketing name myLogger) or i-gotU GT-600 and cycle computer Atech K29. No map is used during the trips.

Implementation Details

  • Uses JavaScript version of Google Maps API (version 3.14)
  • Uses JSON in JavaScript to store trips data
  • Uses Mark McClure's PolylineEncoder class in trips data generator
  • KKJ coordinates were converted to EUREF89 (WGS84) using EurefMuunnos
